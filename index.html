Did you know?

GIS Day

GIS Day is an annual event celebrated on the third Wednesday of November to raise awareness about GIS technology and its applications. It includes workshops, seminars, and community events aimed at showcasing the power of GIS in various fields.

GIS In Sports Analytics

GIS is increasingly used in sports analytics to analyze player performance, optimize team strategies, and enhance fan engagement. It helps teams visualize spatial patterns of play, track player movements, and identify areas for improvement.

Mapping The Internet

GIS can be used to map and visualize internet infrastructure, including data centers, fiber optic cables, and network nodes. This helps optimize internet connectivity and plan for future expansion of digital infrastructure.
